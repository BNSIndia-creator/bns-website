CSR Partnership

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) partnerships enable organizations to create meaningful and measurable community impact.

Bharatiya Navchetana Sangh (BNS) collaborates with corporate partners to design and implement social development initiatives that support education, environmental sustainability, and community empowerment.

CSR Focus Areas

Youth Skill Development

Programs that build leadership, employability, and professional skills among youth.

Education Support Programs

Academic support initiatives including mentoring and night learning programs.

Digital Safety Awareness

Cyber safety and responsible digital citizenship awareness sessions.

Gender Sensitivity Training

Prevention of Sexual Harassment awareness and respectful workplace training.

Environmental Sustainability

Tree plantation drives, waste management awareness, and sustainability programs.

Community Cleanliness Initiatives

Clean city programs promoting sanitation awareness and community participation.

Benefits for CSR Partners

  • Measurable and transparent social impact
  • Employee volunteering opportunities
  • Meaningful community engagement
  • Alignment with national development goals

CSR partners receive regular monitoring reports and clear impact metrics for supported initiatives.

Impact Monitoring

BNS follows transparent monitoring and evaluation processes to measure program outcomes.

Key indicators include:

  • Number of students trained
  • Awareness programs conducted
  • Environmental activities completed
  • Community beneficiaries reached
  • Volunteer participation levels

Partner With Us

Organizations interested in supporting community development through CSR can collaborate with Bharatiya Navchetana Sangh to implement impactful social initiatives.
