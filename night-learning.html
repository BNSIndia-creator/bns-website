Night Learning Program

Expanding Educational Opportunities for Every Student

As part of its Education for All initiative, Bharatiya Navchetana Sangh (BNS) conducts Night Learning Programs to support students who require additional academic guidance and structured learning opportunities beyond regular school hours.

These programs are designed to help students strengthen their academic foundation, build confidence, and prepare for higher education and competitive examinations.

The Night Learning Program focuses particularly on students from communities where access to quality academic support may be limited.

Program Objectives

  • Strengthen foundational learning in core academic subjects
  • Improve understanding of science, mathematics, and language
  • Support students preparing for competitive examinations
  • Build confidence and academic discipline among learners
  • Provide structured educational support after school hours

Through structured mentoring and guided learning sessions, students move closer to their academic and career aspirations.

Key Learning Areas

English Language Development

  • Reading and comprehension
  • Grammar and vocabulary development
  • Spoken English and communication skills
  • Writing and presentation skills

Science & Mathematics

Standards VIII – XII

  • Concept-based teaching in physics, chemistry, and mathematics
  • Problem-solving practice sessions
  • Doubt-clearing and revision classes
  • Preparation for school and board examinations

Competitive Exam Preparation

  • JEE (Joint Entrance Examination)
  • NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)
  • Public service and government recruitment exams

Students receive exam awareness, study strategies, and career guidance.

Program Format

  • Conducted after regular school hours
  • Held in community centers or educational institutions
  • Small group learning formats
  • Guided by volunteer educators and mentors

The program emphasizes discipline, mentorship, and continuous learning support.

Target Beneficiaries

  • Students from Standards VIII to XII
  • Students preparing for board examinations
  • Youth aspiring for competitive entrance examinations
  • Students requiring additional academic support

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved academic performance
  • Greater confidence and motivation in learning
  • Stronger foundation in science and mathematics
  • Encouragement for higher education and professional careers

Support the Night Learning Program

Educational institutions, community groups, and corporate CSR partners can collaborate with BNS to expand access to quality education through the Night Learning Program.

Together, we can help students unlock their potential and build a brighter future.
